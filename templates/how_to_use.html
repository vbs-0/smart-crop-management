How to Use Guide

1 Enter Soil Parameters

Input your soil's nitrogen (N), phosphorus (P), potassium (K), and other environmental parameters in the respective fields.

2 Get Predictions

Click the "Predict Soil" button to receive crop recommendations and nutrient deficiency analysis based on your input parameters.

1 Upload Leaf Image

Click on the upload area to select and upload a clear image of the plant leaf you want to analyze.

2 Analyze Disease

Click the "Predict Image" button to get an analysis of any diseases present in the leaf image.

1 Complete All Inputs

Fill in all soil parameters AND upload a leaf image for comprehensive analysis.

2 Get Complete Analysis

Click the "Predict Hybrid" button to receive a complete analysis including crop recommendations, disease detection, and nutrient deficiencies.
